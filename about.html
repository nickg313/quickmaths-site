About us

Built by someone who's been there.

QuickMaths isn't just another tutoring platform. It's the resource I wish I had when I was struggling through A-Levels myself.

My story.

When I was doing my A-Levels, I hit walls that no textbook or traditional tutor could help me break through. Concepts that seemed straightforward in class suddenly fell apart when I sat down with a past paper at 10pm. I was stuck grinding through material that never appeared in exams, and stuck again when I couldn't get answers at the moment I actually needed them.

I pushed through, went on to study maths at university, and graduated with a BSc in Applied Mathematics. But the frustration of those A-Level years never left me.

That's why I built QuickMaths. It's the platform I wish had existed when I was preparing for my exams: laser-focused on what actually shows up in A Level papers, available 24/7 so you're never stuck waiting for help, and built around the real problems students face — not a generic one-size-fits-all tutoring model.

Every walkthrough, every piece of content, and every feature inside QuickMaths is shaped by one question: "What would have actually helped me?" That's the bar. And that's why our founding members are already getting real results.
